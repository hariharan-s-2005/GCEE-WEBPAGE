About Us

A Legacy of Technical Leadership

Established in 1984 under the Tamil Nadu State Transport Corporation (TNSTC), Government College of Engineering, Erode — formerly known as IRTT — was founded with a noble mission: to provide quality engineering education to the underprivileged.

In 2021, the college was transferred to the Directorate of Technical Education (DoTE) and now serves as the Zonal Headquarters for Zone-XI (Erode Zone) of Anna University, monitoring affiliated colleges across Erode, Namakkal, Tiruppur, and Salem districts.

Situated 15 km from Erode Railway Station on NH-47, between Chithode and Bhavani, our sprawling 200-acre green campus is a hub of academic excellence, innovation, and research.

Exam Cell

Examinations & Results

The Examination Cell manages all aspects of internal and university examinations, results, certificates, and academic records for all students.

Academic Calendar

Odd Semester (July – November)

Classes begin July. Internal Assessment Tests in September & October. End-semester exams in November.

Even Semester (January – May)

Classes resume January. Internal tests in March & April. End-semester university exams in May.

Internal Assessment

Two Cycle Tests per semester. Average of best performances. Minimum 75% attendance mandatory for exam eligibility.

Anna University Exams

Theory and practical examinations conducted as per Anna University, Chennai schedule and guidelines.

Results & Certificates

Results declared on Anna University portal. Original degree certificates issued by Anna University, Chennai.
